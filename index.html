Jedi Knights

Obi Wan Kenobi

Obi-Wan is a legendary Jedi Knight known for his wisdom, patience, and skill with a lightsaber. He plays a central role in the Star Wars saga, serving as a mentor to Anakin Skywalker and later to Luke Skywalker, guiding them in the ways of the Force and standing as a symbol of hope and resilience in the fight against the Sith.
